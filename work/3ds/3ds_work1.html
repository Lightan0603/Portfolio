    Inside Us - Healing

    The “healing process” is a long and difficult journey with psychological, emotional and spiritual aspects. It takes place through psychological stages in humans when suffering from emotional disorders. This is a complex process consisting of many stages from “denial”, “resentment”, “depression” to “acceptance” and ‘’healing’’. Therefore, self-knowledge will be extremely important to clarify psychological wounds and promote the development of recovery.

    The project was created to give readers different perspectives on the psychological recovery process and more empathy for their own emotions.
