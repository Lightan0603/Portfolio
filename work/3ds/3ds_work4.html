    Noise Cancelling Rom

    This work was inspired by the concept of a noise-canceling room, with its concave triangles designed to gradually eliminate sound. In today's noisy world, the invention of soundproofing devices has become increasingly popular.

    However, while noise cancellation can be beneficial, it's important to use it thoughtfully. Overuse or misuse can have unintended negative effects on those around us.
