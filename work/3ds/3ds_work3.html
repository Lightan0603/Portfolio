    DONUT - What The Pink

    This is a work I did when I first learned how to use Blender on Youtube. It gave me a lot of practice in modeling, lighting, and even Geometry Nodes.

    The inspiration for the colors came from visiting various donut shops. I noticed that while the top of the donuts was vibrant and full of different flavors, the bottom was usually the sameâ€”plain cake or lightly sprinkled with sugar. This made me wonder: what if the bottom layer could be just as creative and colorful as the top? This work is my exploration of that idea.
