    Brush With Love

    This advertisement for the toothpaste brand 'Marvis' was designed for Valentine's Day to promote and launch a new flavor. I utilized a collage art style, blending familiar visual elements to craft images that are both relatable and creatively engaging. The use of pink and black in various shades adds a touch of simplicity while effectively highlighting the new toothpaste flavor.
