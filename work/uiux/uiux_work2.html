    BEP LUA POEM

    This is a website I made to present the poem "Bep lua", a famous work by Bang Viet.

    I want to recreate an excerpt from the poem "Bep lua" with images, interactions, animations and sounds to help users receive the verses in many different ways besides just reading and to help improve the user experience. This is also an opportunity to spread traditional folk images of the landscape and people of ancient Vietnam, thereby helping people sympathize and feel emotions from every little thing around them.
